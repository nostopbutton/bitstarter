About us

We founded AURZA out of a frustration of never being able to find the clothes we really wanted. After having children clothes shopping had lost its sparkle, left us deflated and there was always a compromise – too short, too baggy, too expensive or just too plain.

So our aim is simple - to allow women like us to create extraordinary clothes that reflect their own sense of style whilst both flattering and fitting each of our unique bodies.

We want everyone to be able to step out with confidence day and night, feeling a million dollars and we know from personal experience that a great outfit can make all the difference.

We are starting by offering utterly fabulous custom made dresses and separates designed by us and perfected by you. You choose the style and simply change the bodice, skirt, sleeves, length, fabric, trims and accessories.

You can then decide whether our unique standard sizing model based on 3d body scanning is right for you or whether you want us to create a bespoke pattern just for you based on your measurements.

Once you have experienced custom from the comfort of your own home you will always want to feel this good.
