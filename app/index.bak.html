Why AURZA exists

The problem - mass produced clothes don't fit everyone. Styles that are available aren't always suitable for our individual tastes or body shapes.

We founded AURZA out of a frustration of never being able to find the clothes we really wanted. After having 5 kids clothes shopping had lost its sparkle, left us deflated and there was always a compromise – too short, too baggy, too expensive or even too plain.

There was always a compromise – too short, too baggy, too expensive or even too plain.

So our aim is simple - to allow women like us to create extraordinary clothes that reflect their own sense of style whilst both flattering and fitting each of our unique bodies. We want everyone to be able to step out with confidence day and night, feeling a million dollars and we know from personal experience that a great outfit can make all the difference.

We are starting by offering utterly fabulous custom made dresses and separates designed by us and perfected by you. You choose the style and simply change the bodice, skirt, sleeves, length, fabric, trims and accessories. You can then decide whether our unique standard sizing model based on 3d body scanning is right for you or whether you want us to create a bespoke pattern just for you based on your measurements.

Once you have experienced custom from the comfort of your own home you will always want to feel this good.

A better fit

At the heart of ‘AURZA’ is our relentless desire to make clothes that flatter and fit. With 5 kids between us (and bodies not quite like they used to be) we know that well-made tailored clothing make an incredible difference between looking hot or not.

we know that well-made tailored clothing make an incredible difference between looking hot or not

Unlike most off the shelf garments available, our designs are based on revolutionary 3D body scanning and pattern making technology that accurately reflect every curve in a women’s body. We have tested each of our silhouettes and sizes not on an industry ‘fit model’ but on real women with wide ranging body shapes to ensure our designs and fabric choices are as flattering as possible.

revolutionary technology that accurately reflects every curve in a women’s body

Finally, we really do get that everyone is unique and if you want custom sizing to match your unique design we can work with our pattern master to adapt our standard sizing especially for you. Long body – no problem! Bigger top than bottom – we can achieve the look of the dress with separate tops and skirts. You share your concerns and we will work through a solution that works for you and even use all of your body measurements for a full bespoke size if that is what it takes.
